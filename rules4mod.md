# 🎩 **SlimeRide Central Staff Handbook**  
*Welcome aboard, Conductor. This ride doesn’t steer itself.*

---

## 🛠️ **Staff Roles & Responsibilities**

### 🚦 **Conductors (Moderators)**
- Monitor chats and voice channels
- Enforce server rules fairly
- Mute, warn, or kick when needed
- Help answer member questions
- Keep the server safe and fun

### 🧭 **Route Planners (Admins / Senior Mods)**
- Oversee the mod team
- Handle escalated issues
- Manage channels, bots, and settings
- Plan server events or updates
- Support the SlimeTube vision

---

## 📋 **Staff Expectations**

### 1. **Be Present & Active**
Show up when you can. Even just checking in once a day helps keep things running.

### 2. **Stay Professional-ish**
Keep your cool, even when things get messy. We’re the ones keeping the bus on the road.

### 3. **Communicate**
Use `#staff-chat` or `#mod-log` to report issues, flag weird behavior, or suggest improvements.

### 4. **No Favoritism**
Friends or not — everyone gets the same rules. Be neutral and fair.

### 5. **Back Each Other Up**
If a mod makes a call, don’t undercut them publicly. If you disagree, talk it out in staff channels.

### 6. **Keep Logs**
Use bots or mod-logs to document warnings, kicks, or bans. Receipts are key when things go off the rails.

---

## 🚨 **Warnings & Discipline**

If a mod abuses their powers, becomes inactive for long stretches without notice, or creates drama:
- Verbal warning (internal)
- Role review or temporary suspension
- Removal if necessary

We hold ourselves to a higher standard — the vibe starts with us.

---

## 🧼 **Moderator Tools**

- **AutoMod** – Filters slurs, spam, and scam links  
- **ModMail** – For private member concerns  
- **/warn**, **/mute**, **/kick**, **/ban** – Know when and how to use each (refer to #mod-commands)  
- **Ticket Tool or Bot** – For managing reports or disputes
