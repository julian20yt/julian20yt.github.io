Phone Setup Guide

Step 1: Understand Your Hardware

Now that you know the hardware features of your phone, it's time to insert your SIM card and battery and power it on.

Step 2: Remove the Back Cover

To take the back cover off, hold your phone with one hand and put your finger in the recess at the top of the cover. Then pull outwards gently to pry it off.

Step 3: Insert the SIM Card and Battery

Inside you will see slots for the SIM card and battery. Slide your SIM card into the slot below the camera lens until it sits flush with the edge of the battery compartment. Now put your battery in, making sure to align the gold prongs of the phone with the contacts on the battery.

Step 4: Replace the Back Cover

Once your battery and SIM card are secure, replace the back cover by pressing on the bottom lip until you hear it snap into place.

Step 5: Charge Your Phone

Before you power on your Nexus S, make sure to charge it fully using the included charger through the micro-USB port for a few hours.
